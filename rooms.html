Accomodations

Our accommodations are designed to blend seamlessly with the environment, offering luxury and comfort while maintaining an eco-conscious footprint. From our king suites to our executive suites, each room provides panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and lake respectively, allowing you to connect with nature from the comfort of your balcony.

In addition there is a board room to hold meetings and training sessions. Within is a lounge area, a pool, an open lake beach with grey crystal sand and a lake view bar and restaurant all in compound.
